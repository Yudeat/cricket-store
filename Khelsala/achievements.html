The Legacy of Cricket in Nepal

Tracing the journey from humble beginnings to international achievements

1964
Nepal Cricket Team in 1964.
1988
Nepal became ICC Affiliate Member.
1996
Upgraded to ICC Associate Member.
1998–2000
Started participating in ACC tournaments.
2001–2010
Rise of youth cricket and U-19 World Cups.
2010–2013
Domestic cricket and coaching improved.
2014
Nepal qualified for ICC T20 World Cup.
2015–2017
Rise of domestic leagues: EPL, DPL.
2018
Nepal gained ODI status & played first ODI.
2019–2021
Consistent participation in global events.
